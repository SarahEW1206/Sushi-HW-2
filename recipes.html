Recipe of the Week

Matcha Ice Cream

By Cookpad.com

Prep Time: 15 Minutes
Yield: 3 Servings
Serving: 1/2 Cup
Calories: 200
Fat: 14 Grams

This matcha ice cream has an amazingly strong flavor of matcha. It is very creamy and is much cheaper to make at home than to buy at the store. This recipe is so simple because it only has three ingredients and you do not need an ice cream machine.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 Cup Condensed Milk
  • 1 Cup Heavy Cream
  • 2 TBS Matcha Powder

Instructions:

  1. Sift the matcha powder into a bowl.
  2. Pour in the heavy cream and the condensed milk and mix all the ingredients on high speed in a stand mixer or a hand mixer until soft peaks form.
  3. Put all of the mixture into an airtight container and freeze for at least 8 hours or overnight.
  4. Eat and enjoy!
